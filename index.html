About Us

Welcome to Serge Auto Clinic, your trusted automotive repair shop in
Clarence-Rockland.


When Gilles Seguin took ownership of Serge Auto Clinic in 2022, he brought with him over 23 years of experience as a master technician, including specialized expertise with Toyota vehicles and extensive knowledge servicing all makes and models. As a proud resident of Rockland for over 25 years, Gilles deeply values the trust and respect he's earned in the community.

Working alongside him is his son Danick, a skilled and passionate mechanic committed to delivering meticulous, reliable automotive care. Together, Gilles and Danick combine their expertise and genuine dedication to ensure every vehicle receives exceptional service and personalized attention.

At Serge Auto Clinic, honesty, quality workmanship, and customer satisfaction are more than promises—they're principles that guide every job we undertake.

We look forward to welcoming you to our shop and providing the outstanding service you've come to expect from Rockland's automotive experts.
